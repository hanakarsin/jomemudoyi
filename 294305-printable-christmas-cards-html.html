    When I was about 12 years old, my mom allowed me to accompany her to the store for Xmas shopping. It was the very first time that she had allowed me to go with her to the store. I was over the moon with excitement. My excitement stemmed from the fact that growing up in the village; I hadn't been to a supermarket before. For me, the trip was not a necessity, but an adventure of a lifetime. I still remember the awe and wonder that I felt when I saw the first live fully lit and decorated Christmas tree. It was like a dream come true. The tree stood magnificently at the door of the grocery store, and I remember my mom dragging me along so I could stop gawking. I brushed my shoulder against the tree, and something fell off two Christmas cards.

    I had seen success cards before, and maybe one get well soon notes. However, I hadn't seen or heard about Xmas designs, or the Xmas pasteboards as as others call them. As I placed them back on the top of the tree, avoiding the warning look from the guard, I remember thinking, “Those families look very happy. I want to have a pasteboard like that to send to my friends one day.” Well, now, almost 25 years later, these paste boards are a huge part of the holiday for me. Here are some pasteboards that I think are the best.

    What should I write in a Christmas card?

    There are some factors which determine what you will write on a festive season card. Before writing the message, consider the following things:

    • Is the person or the people you are sending the pasteboard to religious?
    • What is the age of the person, and how close are you to them?
    • Do they have a sense of humor?

    Weight these factors when crafting a message. You can also search for Christmas quotes and messages to go with the pasteboards that you create.

    How much do Xmas cards cost?

    There is no universal price or price range for Xmas pasteboards. However, most of them cost between 50-1000 Kenyan shillings. There are some that are high-end and highly customized. These may cost a lot more than the regular designs, but they highly personalized, pleasant and worth every penny that you spend on them.
