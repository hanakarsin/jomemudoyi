    Sports journalist Carol Radull is one of the best there is in Kenya having done what she does best for over a decade.

    Radull who has been a presenter at Kiss 100 and Classic 105 for many years loves sports and this she has proven by the many times she has come out to assist our teams.

    Be it football, basketball, volleyball or even athletics, Radull has been at the forefront in ensuring the national teams get all the needed support.

    Still in that spirit, Radull now wants to do something even bigger for her people back in the village.

    TUKO.co.ke has learnt the veteran sports journalist has a dream of converting one of her many farms (shambas) into a football pitch where people can practise and even host big football competition.

    This was first revealed by Radull herself who shared a photo of a dugout shamba, followed by hints that one of her farms will one day be turned into a playing field.

    Speaking in an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke, Radull confirmed indeed she is hoping to convert her shamba into a football pitch.

    Her move is very generous and somewhat a big deal, but the sports guru said it is just a small token, terming it as ''just a field with goalposts''.

    ''It is my dream for it to happen in the near future. But keep in mind that a football pitch is just a flat piece of land with goalposts. Not a stadium.''' Radull told TUKO.co.ke.

    Radull is known for her big heart and helping spirit.

    She has severally come out to ask Kenyans to help their sportsmen and women with boxer Conjestina being one of her success stories.
