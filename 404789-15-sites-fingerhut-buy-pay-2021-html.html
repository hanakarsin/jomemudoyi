    Afterpay works with hundreds of retail stores to allow you to shop as usual and choose the option to pay later. When you get financing with Afterpay, you can repay the amount in four instalments. The payments are due every two weeks and do not attract any interests.

    You also have the option of changing the due date of the payment if you are unable to repay on time. But that will depend on the item you bought. Whenever you pay on time, Afterpay does not charge you any interest or additional fees. You can apply for the financing and get approved instantly.

    2. Affirm

    Affirm allows you to shop for an item from hundreds of stores and pay later in instalments. The company provides you with point-of-sale loans that you can pay off in fixed monthly payments.     Klarna offers the best alternative to credit and gives users the flexibility of shopping what they want. The company allows you to buy an item and pay later. You will then split the cost of your purchase into four payments. Each payment is due every two weeks and does not incur any interests.
