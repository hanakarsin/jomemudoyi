    Wizkid, Tems Win Best Collaboration In 2021 Soul Train Awards

    The song which has been nominated in the 2021 Grammy Awards has received reviews all over the world. The talented singer in the song which featured Tems clinched the Best Collaboration award at the Apollo Theatre in New York on Sunday night.

    24 May 2024, 00:00

    Award-winning Nigerian singer, Wizkid has won the award for the best collaboration at the 2021 Soul Train Awards with his song featuring Tems, Essence.

    The song which has been nominated in the 2021 Grammy Awards has received reviews all over the world.

    The talented singer in the song which featured Tems clinched the Best Collaboration award at the Apollo Theatre in New York on Sunday night.

    The song was nominated in other categories, however, it was not strong enough to beat the winner to clinch the awards in those categories.

    The Soul Train award celebrates the best in African-American culture, music, and entertainment.
