    The tea is piping hot in Season 10 of Bravo's Vanderpump Rules, but it's the illicit affair between Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss that happened off-camera that's making headlines these days.

    In early March, we learned that Tom had cheated on his longtime girlfriend, Ariana Madix, with his Vanderpump Rules co-star. The news broke our hearts — and the internet — but Tom and Raquel’s steamy saga didn’t end there. In fact, there were still several more entanglements to come.

    Along with rumors suggesting that the could-be couple are starting a life together, there’s also speculation that they might be expecting a bundle of joy. So, is Raquel pregnant? Here’s what we know …

    Is Raquel Leviss from ‘Vanderpump Rules’ pregnant?

    First of all, what a woman does with her womb is none of our business. However … we have all been dying to know how the rest of this starcrossed love story unfolds. After all, Tom and Raquel have both expressed their desire to get married and have kids — why not do it together? That seems to be exactly what lots of people were thinking with the rumors that Raquel is pregnant.

    In a now-deleted TikTok video, a TikTok user claimed that she had received a message from someone who knows Raquel's sister. They alleged that Raquel is pregnant and "hiding out" at her grandmother's house in Tucson, Ariz (per PageSix).

    As you might expect, the TikTok quickly went viral — however, multiple sources have now confirmed that Raquel is not pregnant, and the rumor was never anything more than just that.

    Keep reading for a look at her and Tom's relationship timeline!

    Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss’s relationship timeline explained.

    While we only recently learned of Tom and Raquel’s secret romance, a source told Page Six that they had been getting it on for months now. An insider shared that they first started hooking up in June of 2022, only months after she broke off her engagement with her boyfriend of five years, Vanderpump Rules star James Kennedy.

    According to an Instagram direct message James sent to Deux Moi (per Cosmopolitan), the two even fooled around in the $2 million home that Tom and Ariana shared while she was asleep.

    Article continues below advertisement

    As if things couldn’t get any messier, Tom also helped James foot the cost of James's proposal to Raquel in late 2021.

    “James and I split the $25K [bill] because nothing brings me more joy than to help a friend accomplish a dream that they have,” Tom previously said in an October 2022 episode of Vanderpump Rules.

    After news of their affair went viral, both Tom and Raquel took to Instagram to issue a public apology. “There is no excuse. I am not a victim, and I must own my actions, and I deeply regret hurting Ariana,” she wrote in a post.

    But she and Tom should beware — Vanderpump fans aren’t quick to forgive.
