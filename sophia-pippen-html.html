    Sophia Pippen - Age, Family, Bio

    Daughter of Hall of Fame NBA forward Scottie Pippen. She was born as part of his second marriage to his wife Larsa. She has posted an array of photographs displaying her luxury lifestyle on her Instagram page.

    03 May 2024, 00:00

    Daughter of Hall of Fame NBA forward Scottie Pippen. She was born as part of his second marriage to his wife Larsa. She has posted an array of photographs displaying her luxury lifestyle on her Instagram page. 

    Before Fame

    Her father rose to prominence during the 1990s as a member of the Chicago Bulls. He won 6 NBA championships playing alongside Michael Jordan during that time period.

    Trivia

    She cultivated a sizable social media following, earning over 150,000 followers to her sophiapippen Instagram account before it was disabled. She has often been featured in photos on her mother's Instagram page alongside North West. In January of 2021, she posted a photo holding a baby tiger on her Instagram. 

    Family Life

    She has 3 biological siblings and 1 half-sibling from her father's first marriage. She has 3 other half-siblings.

    Associated With

    Her mother is friends with Kim Kardashian, and is often seen out with her family.
