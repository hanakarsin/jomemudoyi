    Funny kids jokes to tell your children

    Children absolutely adore laughing, and they would quickly get attached to an adult who would tell them some funny kids jokes. Everyone who regularly communicates with youngsters needs to learn some good jokes for kids to entertain them and make them smile.

    Children absolutely adore laughing, and they would quickly get attached to an adult who would tell them some funny kids jokes. Everyone who regularly communicates with youngsters needs to learn some good jokes for kids to entertain them and make them smile.

    There is a large variety of funny jokes for kids, including kids knock knock jokes, amusing puns and plenty of other things that can make your child giggle. There is always a chance that your youngsters will groan after hearing them because some of them are very cheesy and corny, but there is just as much of a chance that they will amuse them.

    Best kids jokes

    These hilarious lines are in a question-answer format. Normally, it works the following way: you ask a question, the child does not know the answer, and you reveal it after a small pause. If everything goes well, the young one will laugh and be amused. Some of them also contain references to popular characters from children's books and the media.

    • What’s faster, cold or hot? - Hot, because you can catch a cold.

    • Where do cows go for entertainment? - To the moo-vies!

    • What do you call a dog magician? - A labracadabrador.

    Knock knock jokes for kids

    All knock-knock lines follow the same structure. You say "Knock, knock!, the person answers "Who’s there?", and you say some words that will later evolve into a pun. The person will have to repeat the words and add "who?". Then, you finish by delivering the punchline. Knock-knock puns are great kid jokes and are usually loved by young children.

    The following examples start with "Knock, knock!" - "Who's there?".

    • Hatch. - Hatch who? - Bless you!
    • Annie. - Annie who? - Annie body home?
    • Boo. - Boo who? - Don’t cry; it’s just a joke!
    • Lettuce. - Lettuce who? - Lettuce in, it’s cold out here!
    • Bean. - Bean who? - Bean a while since I saw you!
    • Orange. - Orange who? - Orange you going to let me in?
    • Adore. - Adore who? - Adore is between us. Open up!
    • Owls say. - Owls say who? - Yes, they do.
    • Kanga. - Kanga who? - Actually, it’s kangaroo.
    • Spell. - Spell who? - W-H-O.
