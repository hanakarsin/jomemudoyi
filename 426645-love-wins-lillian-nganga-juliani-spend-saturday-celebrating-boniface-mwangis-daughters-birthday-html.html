    It is no longer a secret that Lillian Nganga and Julius Owino popularly known as Juliani are dating.

    The two lovebirds have been hanging out together in public of late. Lillian parted ways with her ex-lover Machakos governor Alfred Mutua in June and publicly announced that they were no longer an item on August 15, 2021.

    In a recent interview, Juliani disclosed that he met with Lilian for the first time in June 2021. He said that he was attracted to her because of her character, and he started to woo her.

    "As a man, I did not sleep in class. I have not seen many women like her. She has clarity of mind, clarity of purpose, the kind of things she values, self-respect," he said.
