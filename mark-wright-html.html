    Reality TV personality and former soccer player who became known for his role on the series The Only Way Is Essex. He is also known for his appearances on Mark Wright's Hollywood Nights and he competed on the 12th series of Strictly Come Dancing, finishing in fourth place with his partner Karen Hauer.

    Before Fame

    He played semi-professional soccer for Tottenham Hotspur's youth team at the age 18.

    Trivia

    He also appeared on I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here and has been a presenter on Take Me Out: The Gossip.

    Family Life

    He is the son of Carol and Mark Wright Sr. He has two sisters, Natalya and Jessica, and one brother, Josh. In 2015, he married actress Michelle Keegan.

    Associated With

    He co-starred on The Only Way is Essex with Amy Childs.
