    Chloride Exide is one of the best companies in East Africa dealing with not only the distribution of automotive batteries but also solar systems, backup systems, and solar water heating systems. The firm has a considerable COMESA block presence with an extensive network and masses of dealers across the region.

    Chloride Exide has significantly grown over time from a single based operator to abundant renewable energy and battery distributor. The company has three establishments in the region. The Kenyan branch was established in 1963, followed by Tanzania in 1966 and Uganda in 2001.

    Suffice it to say that they know everything to do with batteries as they have been in the market for quite an extended period. Here is everything you need to know about Chloride Exide shops, inverters, batteries, contacts and much more.

    Services offered by Chloride Exide

    The company is certified to operate in the region. It aims at complying, maintaining, and continuously improving the effectiveness of quality management systems. Their main products include the following:

    Their products are manufactured to the highest quality and standards by the Associated Battery Manufacturers Limited. In addition to this, their vision “Energy Solutions for all” and mission “to provide quality batteries, solar panels, and related solutions while constantly exceeding consumer expectations and ensuring market expansion” are their driving force.

    The company also runs an online shopping platform where you can purchase various cells and have them delivered at your doorstep conveniently. The service is available in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru. Future expansions might accommodate the rest of the towns in the country.
