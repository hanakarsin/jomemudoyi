    20 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Former Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Housemate Pere Egbi has surprised many of his colleagues as he splashes millions on a new ride despite winning a car during the reality TV show.

    Recall that just five days to the end of the BBNaija show, Pere Egbi won a brand new IVM Innoson car for emerging the first position in a house task.

    However, Pere seemed to have dumped the car he won as he acquires a new car worth millions of Naira.

    The reality TV star shared a video of the new car on his page saying that the post doesn’t require a caption.

    As expected many of his colleagues such as Whitemoney, Tega, Ford, and Boma including some fans congratulated Pere on his new car.
