    Wedding anniversary blessings and prayers have hitherto been used as a mean of expressing one's great wishes to a lovely couple during their anniversary.

    A wedding anniversary is the anniversary of the day a wedding took place. Some couples like to hold parties to commemorate this day. You can crown the day by inviting God's presence into their family using prayers.

    Wedding anniversary prayers of the faithful Catholic

    The Bible challenges us to seek God's intervention through prayers. Prayers are communications with God and are said for different reasons. For a wedding anniversary, you can grace the occasion with any of the prayers below.

    O Lord, omnipotent and eternal God, we give you thanks, and we bless your holy name. You created man and woman in your image and blessed their union so that each would be for the other a help and support. Remember us today. Protect us and grant that our love may be in the image of the devotion and love of Christ for his Church. Grant us a long and fruitful life together, in joy and peace, so that, through your Son and in the Holy Spirit, our hearts may always rise to you in praise and goods works.

    Thank you message to God for our wedding anniversary

    Very few marriages survive to see the next anniversary. You, therefore, have to be grateful to God for seeing you through the years. Below is a wedding anniversary prayer for the wife and husband.

    O Lord, my Father, thank you that you are our defender and our strength. You have promised that you will never forsake us, you are always there for us. You have put us jointly as one flesh. Assist us to work collectively in our marriage. May we be here for each other, encourage each other and serve one another. Please help us to love each other in the same sacrificial way you have loved us. You are the Father of hope. Fill us with all joy and peace in believing, so that by the power of the Holy Spirit, we may abound in hope. Through Jesus Christ, your Son, Amen.

    Prayers for married couples

    You can express your good wishes to a married couple through prayers. Pray for their happiness and fulfillment using some of these prayers below.

    God of love, may we be filled with love for each other that reflects your love for us. Your love is patient. Let us be patient with each other. Your love is kind, let us be kind to each other. Your love does not envy, let us not be envious of each other. Your love does not boast, may our relationship be built on humility. You love does not dishonor others, let us honor each other. Your love is not self-seeking. Let us love each other with selfless love. Help us to love each other well. Through Jesus Christ our Lord, AmenMay God be the co-author of every plan developed in your marriage. May he be the director of all you set to do.
