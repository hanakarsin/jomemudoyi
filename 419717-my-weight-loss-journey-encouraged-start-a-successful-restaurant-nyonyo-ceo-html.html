    A woman identified as Chichi Yakubu has inspired people after revealing that she started her own restaurant Nyo Nyo after seeing positive results from her weight loss routine.

    Her restaurant is a fast-growing popular restaurant located in East Legon, American House in Ghana, and it has more than 130,000 followers on Instagram.

    In a Youtube video, Yakubu narrated how she got into the food business and what that meant for her.

    The inspiration behind the business

    This businesswoman explained that what inspired her to start her food business was her weight.

    She elucidated that she was struggling with self-confidence and desired to lose weight which then led to proper meal planning for herself.

    When her friends noticed her body changes, they started to inquire what her weight loss routine was and how they could try it.

    Making the move

    Realising how her eating plan positively impacted her weight loss journey, she sent word out to her friends about her availability to meal-prep for them at a fee if they are interested.

    However, when she started her own restaurant, she did not quit her day job immediately.

    Rather, she juggled between the two to be sure venturing into the food business would pay off and could sustain her livelihood.

    She worked both jobs for one and a half years and was finally convinced NyoNyo Gh had become a successful business that could employ other people as well.
