    In a video shared by Boksburg Advertiser, authorities were seen struggling to put out the raging fire that had completely consumed the pick-up truck.

    Apparently, the lady's husband stepped into the station to report abuse he had allegedly suffered in the hands of his wife.

    It is understood the unnamed lady was arrested and charged with destruction of property shortly after the daring incident.

    According to North South African Police Service (SAPS), Constable Justice Ramaube, the couple was involved in an argument prior to the incident.

    “The couple apparently had an argument at home before the husband decided that they both go to the police station to seek help,” he said.

    He added the husband's vehicle was completely destroyed by the massive fire.
