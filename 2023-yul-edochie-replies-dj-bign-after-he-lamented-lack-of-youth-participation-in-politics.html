    Yul Edochie Replies DJ BigN After He Lamented Lack Of Youth Participation In Politics

    19 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Ace Nigerian movie star and filmmaker, Yul Edochie, has reaffirmed his interest in running for the presidential seat in 2023 following DJ Big N’s outburst on lack of youth participation in politics. 

    Naija News earlier reported that Disc Jockey, Big N recently admonished the youths to come out with their own representatives as the 2023 general elections draw near.

    According to him, the youths must wake up and make plans as older politicians are already gearing up for the forthcoming elections.

    He also added that they need not complain about wherever things end up if they don’t make a collective effort to come up with their own representatives.

    Reacting to his post, Yul Edochie via his Instagram reaffirmed his interest to run for the seat of the presidency.

    He also noted that he had declared his interest months ago, urging the DJ to support his ambition as a youth actively involved and participating in Nigerian politics. 

    Read his post below; ‘;My dear brother @djbign I saw your post this morning about the youths not presenting a candidate yet.

    Thank you so much for your concern.

    My name is Yul Edochie and I’m running for President of Nigeria in 2023.

    I declared my interest months ago.

    I humbly ask for your support.

    I believe that by posting me on your page, putting in a few words and supporting us through every other means at your disposal our movement will gain more momentum, and certainly many others will join.

    Victory awaits us.

    Thank you brother.

    God bless you.

    God bless Nigeria.”
