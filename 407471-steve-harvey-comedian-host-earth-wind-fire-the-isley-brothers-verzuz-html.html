    Steve Harvey has proven to the world he still got it after landing yet another huge hosting Verzuz battle.

    The world-acclaimed TV host and radio personality will be the host for the upcoming Verzuz battle between legendary RnB groups Earth, Wind & Fire and The Isley Brothers, TUKO.co.ke has learnt.

    This is quite a show for Steve Harvey having been the biggest Earth, Wind & Fire fan.

    TUKO.co.ke understands the TV host has always been paying tribute to the Earth, Wind & Fire crew and their music throughout his career and comedy routine.
