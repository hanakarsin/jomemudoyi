    Machachari actress Mama Baha dismisses death rumours

    Celebrated Machachari actress Wanjiku Mburu, known to many as Mama Baha, has dispelled rumours that she is no more. Mama Baha while speaking to TUKO.co.ke on Wednesday, October 2, condemned those spreading the rumours, confirming she was alive and well.

    01 Jan 0001, 00:00

    Celebrated Machachari actress Wanjiku Mburu, known to many as Mama Baha, has dispelled rumours that she is no more.

    Mama Baha while speaking to TUKO.co.ke on Wednesday, October 2, condemned those spreading the rumours, confirming she was alive and well.

    Mama Baha said the confusion arose after the death of Pamela Wanjiru, wife of Machachari's director of photography, Mburu Kimani.

    ''I am well. The blogs should at least call people to confirms before spreading false reports. It is true someone died but it is not me. I am not happy with the false reports and people using my photos. My heart goes out to Mburu Kimani and his family for the loss of their loved one," she told TUKO.co.ke.

    READ ALSO: News anchor Hassan Mugambi clinches prestigious investigative journalism award

    ''My fans should know I am alive and let us keep doing our work,'' she added.

    Mama Baha's friends also took to social media to clear the air, stating the actress was alive and well, and that a confusion arose following Mburu's wife's death announcement.

    ''For clarification purposes, the one who passed on is Mburu Kimani's wife. Mburu is the director of photography for Machachari. Some artists who wanted us to break the news gave us wrong information saying it was Mama Baha. I apologise to Mama Baha and her entire family and fans. To Mburu Kimani, our sincere condolences for the loss,'' one Emily Princess Kimani said.

    Mama Baha came out to rubbish the claims hours after a number of Kenyan blogs reported she had succumbed to cancer.

    According to the fake reports, she allegedly died on Tuesday, October 1.
