We Igbos Are Undoing Ourselves, Killing Ourselves

13 May 2024, 00:00

The Ebonyi State Governor, Dave Umahi has observed that the Igbos are the very ones responsible for the challenges facing them as a people. Umahi who made the submission during the World Igbo Day celebration at the Christian Ecumenical Centre, Abakaliki, said it is time to look inwards and avoid further crises than what the region has already suffered. He observed that the forefathers paid a great price for the freedom and peace in the region but some people who are not students of history seem to be rubbishing the labours of the founding fathers because of their selfish interests.
