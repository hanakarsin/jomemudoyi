PoS Operator Foils Customers Attempt To Make N90,000 Transaction With Fake Notes

10 Mar 2024, 00:00

A Point-of-Sale (PoS) operator in Lagos State has successfully foiled a customer’s attempt to carry out a transaction using counterfeit naira notes. The customer, identified as Linda Ihebinike, attempted to use counterfeit ₦1,000 notes to complete a transaction totalling N90,000. The PoS operator’s alertness played a crucial role in preventing the fraudulent activity. Naija News reports that the State’s Police Public Relations Officer, SP Benjamin Hundeyin, shared details of the incident on his X (formerly Twitter) handle on Sunday, commending the PoS operator’s vigilance.
